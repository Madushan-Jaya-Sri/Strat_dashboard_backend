Momentro Pvt Ltd

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 22, 2024

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Momentro Pvt Ltd ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and deletes Platform Data and other data for the internal Momentro dashboard hosted at eyqi6vd53z.us-east-2.awsapprunner.com.

This dashboard is for internal use only (employees and authorized contractors) and is not offered to the public.

Definitions

Platform Data — data obtained from Meta APIs (Graph API) such as Ads Insights, Campaign metrics, Page/Instagram metrics, audience aggregates, and metadata tied to business accounts. This does not include the items excluded by Platform Terms 3(e).

What Data We Collect

  • Platform Data from Meta Graph API (ads insights, campaign stats, page/instagram metrics) — only as permitted by granted permissions.
  • Account/email used to access the dashboard (for authentication and audit).
  • Operational logs and performance metrics (for debugging and platform health).

How We Use Platform Data

Platform Data is used only to render internal reports, perform analytics, monitor campaign performance, and support internal business decision-making. No advertising or data sharing outside authorized internal users.

Storage, Caching & Transfer

All Platform Data is stored in our secure cloud database(s). We do not store Platform Data on organizational or personal devices (laptops, phones, removable drives). Data in transit uses HTTPS/TLS. Access to stored Platform Data is restricted by role-based access controls and MFA for permitted accounts.

Retention and Deletion

We retain Platform Data only as long as it is necessary for legitimate business purposes (e.g., to display dashboard metrics, run internal reports, or comply with legal/audit obligations). Typical retention periods are:

  • Cached/processed dashboard data: up to 90 days by default (configurable).
  • Audit logs: up to 1 year or as required by regulation.

If Platform Data is no longer necessary, it is deleted or irreversibly anonymized.

User Data Deletion Requests

If an internal user (or an authorized account owner) requests deletion of Platform Data associated with their account, they may request it by:

  1. Using the dashboard: Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete My Data, which triggers automated deletion for the account's Platform Data; or
  2. Emailing support at madushan.jayasri@momentro.com with the account identifier. We will authenticate the request and delete associated Platform Data within 30 days unless legal hold applies.
Note: Platform Data listed in Platform Terms 3(e) are excluded from this policy per Meta's definitions.

Security

We maintain industry-standard protections: encrypted storage at rest, HTTPS/TLS for all network traffic, role-based access control, MFA for privileged accounts, and periodic security patching of code and infrastructure.

Third Parties

We do not share Platform Data with third-party vendors except where required for hosting, backups, or tooling. Any such provider is contractually required to maintain equivalent data protection and only process data on our instruction.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
